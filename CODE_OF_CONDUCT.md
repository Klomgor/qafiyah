# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or beliefs. This project values the dignified treatment of all individuals in accordance with principles of mutual respect and professional conduct.

## Our Standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive and productive environment includes:

- Demonstrating respect and courtesy in all interactions
- Using inclusive and professional language
- Accepting constructive feedback with grace and humility
- Focusing on the collective benefit of the community
- Showing empathy and understanding toward others
- Maintaining the integrity and dignity of discourse

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments of any kind
- Trolling, insulting remarks, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Disclosure of private information without explicit consent
- Sexual imagery, inappropriate advances, or unwelcome attention
- Disrespectful or profane language
- Any conduct that violates Islamic principles of respectful interaction
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communications related to the project, both online and in person.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the authority and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. They may also temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by contacting **alwalxed@proton.me**. All reports will be reviewed promptly, handled with discretion, and kept confidential.
